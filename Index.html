About Warner Bros.

Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc., a WarnerMedia company, is a prominent figure in the entertainment industry, excelling in various forms of entertainment across traditional and emerging media platforms. The studio boasts a highly successful array of brands globally and leads the way in feature film, television, home entertainment production, and worldwide distribution. Additionally, Warner Bros. is actively involved in digital distribution, animation, comic books, video games, product and brand licensing, and broadcasting, positioning itself at the forefront of the entertainment sector.
